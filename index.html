Sustainable and Efficient Coconut Oil Industry in Sri Lanka

Sri Lanka, known for its lush coconut plantations, ranks as the 4th largest producer of coconuts and the 7th largest producer of coconut oil globally. The coconut oil industry plays a significant role in the nation's economy, offering various products ranging from refined oil to virgin coconut oil. However, with the rise of global demand and dynamic market conditions, it is essential to adopt innovative methods to boost productivity, sustainability, and efficiency in this sector.

The rapid evolution of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) provides new opportunities for transformation across industries, including agriculture and manufacturing. Applying these technologies can enhance the coconut oil industry by optimizing production, forecasting market demands, and ensuring product quality. Additionally, these technologies can empower manufacturers to make data-driven decisions about product types and target markets, supporting better strategic planning and export potential.

This research focuses on exploring how AI/ML tools can be effectively implemented to address key challenges and unlock new possibilities for the Sri Lankan coconut oil industry. Specifically, it seeks to develop insights into future demand predictions based on past trends, introduce rapid quality testing systems via smartphones, and offer targeted marketing strategies for exports. The findings aim to help manufacturers improve efficiency, enhance product quality, and align their operations with global market needs while fostering sustainable growth.

Domain Overview

Literature Survey

The literature survey highlights gaps in the current research on coconut oil production, especially regarding advanced predictive models and machine learning integration. This study aims to bridge these gaps by introducing AI-driven predictive tools to enhance the industry's productivity and quality control.

Proposed Problem

This research addresses the inefficiencies in Sri Lanka's coconut oil industry due to reliance on manual processes and lack of predictive tools. Integrating AI/ML technologies will enhance productivity, improve market prediction accuracy, and increase international competitiveness.

Proposed Solution

We propose a web application using machine learning algorithms to predict demand, assess product quality, and identify profitable export markets. This application aims to streamline production, enhance export potential, and support small-scale producers by removing inefficiencies.

Main Objective

The objective is to improve sustainability and efficiency in Sri Lanka's coconut oil production. By developing predictive models, this research empowers small-scale producers, optimizes production processes, and provides real-time data insights to enhance profitability and direct market access.

Methodology

The methodology includes data collection, machine learning model development (e.g., CNN for quality prediction), and integrating these models into a user-friendly web application for decision support. Continuous monitoring ensures adaptability to market changes.

Research Gap

This research identifies a lack of predictive tools for Sri Lanka's coconut oil industry and limited real-time quality assessment systems. By introducing AI-driven models, the study addresses these gaps, enhancing production and distribution strategies for maximum profitability.

Our Key Features

Smart Forecasting Solutions to Optimize Coconut Oil Production and Export Strategies

Supply and Demand Predictive Model

Build predictive models for forecasting export volumes. Use logistic regression for price forecasting and random forest regression for optimal export volume prediction, ensuring high accuracy in market forecasts.

Coconut Oil Quality Predictive Model

Leverage hybrid models combining CNNs and decision trees to assess coconut oil quality. This approach ensures high accuracy and helps streamline industrial quality control processes.

Copra Quality Predictive Model

Use Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) to classify copra quality in real-time. Deployed via a mobile app, this solution offers instant feedback, enhancing product quality control for producers.

Oil Product Estimator

This estimator uses machine learning to optimize oil production by predicting input needs and yield. A decision tree model tracks and forecasts production based on market demands and environmental conditions.
